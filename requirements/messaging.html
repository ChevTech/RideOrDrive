Messaging

Title: Messaging

Actor: A user who has made a post and another user who wants to respond to the post. Primarily to help communication between riders and drivers.

Prerequisites: One of the users must have posted a ride request or a drive offer.

Action: Type a message in a texbox.

Effect: Sends the message to the other user.

Next: Sends a notification to the other user.
