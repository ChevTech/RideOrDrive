Rate rider

Title: Rate your rider after the ride.

Actor: The driver can rate the rider.

Prerequisites: The rider must have asked for a ride.

Action: Click a Rate button that allows the driver to give a rating for the user with optional comments.

Effect: This will create a rating or update the rating for the rider. (Each client can have both a driver rating and rider rating)

Next: Posts a notification on the rider's wall.
